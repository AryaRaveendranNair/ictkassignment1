THIRUVANANTHAPURAM

A Tropical Paradise

Kovalam

A beach lover’s paradise, Kovalam is a small coastal town in Thiruvananthapuram, the capital of Kerala. With its three crescent-shaped beaches and the cultural, heritage, wellness, zen and adventure sports experiences, Kovalam is the ideal destination to enjoy one’s vacation.Life begins with a surreal vibrance at Kovalam. Whether you choose to laze away with your favourite drink and stare into the horizon amid fishing activities, to scuttle through the alleyways experiencing the myriad cultures or get adventurous by indulging in some sporty activities, Kovalam is a melting pot of experiences waiting to be explored by the traveller

Veli Tourist Village

A day full of fun and frolic, that’s what the Veli Tourist Village will do for you. There is never a dull moment here with it being one of the go-to picnic spots in the city. Be it lazing away on the golden sands and watching the sun dip into the horizon or pedaling through the placid waters of the lake, Veli Tourist Village has everything to satiate the wanderlust in you.The village lies where the Veli Lake meets the Arabian Sea. Marvel at the sculptures or, better, indulge in the many recreational activities on offer at the tourist village. Plenty of boating opportunities await travellers here.

Sree Padmanabhaswami Temple

Located inside the magnificent fort complex in Thiruvananthapuram, the Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu.The wealthiest temple in the whole world, where gemstones, coins and other treasures to the tune of trillions of dollars lie in the vaults. One of the ancient temples in the state, the Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple which blends the Chera and Dravidian styles of architecture evokes a sense of wonderment among anyone with a penchant for history and lores

Ponmudi

If all you seek is to escape into the mountains, then Ponmudi is your best bet. A narrow, winding trail leads you to this famed hill station in Kerala.The hill station of Ponmudi is the getaway destination for every hiker in the city. With its mist wrapped peak, lush green surrounds, panoramic vista of rolling hills and an ethereal aura, Ponmudi located in the majestic Western Ghats is your ideal green hideout away from the din of the city. Be it the constant cackle of the birds or the occasional sighting of the wild beings, Ponmudi has everything in store for the nature enthusiast in you.

Neyyar Dam & Park

The Neyyar Dam is a picturesque picnic spot that is frequented by locals and travellers alike. It is part of the Neyyar Wildlife Sanctuary, a verdant oasis, which boasts of a lush 12,000 hectares of natural vegetation. The rich flora and fauna of the sanctuary along with the many trekking trails is any nature enthusiast’s dream come true.The Watch Tower, Deer Park, Lion Safari Park and Crocodile Rehabilitation and Research Centre at Neyyar offer a wholesome travel experience replete with fun and blissful moments.. Hop into the boat and slide through the tranquil waters, experiencing the jungle tales.

Shangumugham Beach

Noted for its relaxed surrounds and its imposing stone sculptures, Shanghumugham is that favourite beach destination in the city. Famous for its glorious sunsets, serene surrounds, golden sun-kissed sands and uninterrupted fun elements, Shanghumugham is where you can build all your sandcastles or fly your kite in abandon. Matsyakanyaka or Jalakanyaka, a gigantic, 35-m long sculpture of a reclining mermaid which appears to have risen from the sea only to spend some time lazing away on the beach, is an iconic structure that continues to induce a sense of awe and wonderment among visitors.
